# ResumeMatch

**AI-powered resume analysis and job matching platform**

Get instant insights into how well your resume matches any job description. Advanced AI analyzes your resume and provides actionable recommendations to improve your chances of getting hired.

** [Try it now - https://resumematchapp.streamlit.app/](https://resumematchapp.streamlit.app/)**

## Features

- **Smart Matching**: AI-powered compatibility scoring (0-100%)
- **Detailed Analysis**: Experience, skills, and education breakdown
- **ATS Check**: Ensure your resume passes applicant tracking systems
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify missing keywords and skills
- **Action Items**: Get specific recommendations to improve
- **Multiple Formats**: Supports PDF and DOCX uploads

## How to Use

1. **Visit [resumematchapp.streamlit.app](https://resumematchapp.streamlit.app/)**
2. Upload your resume (PDF or DOCX)
3. Paste the job description
4. Click "Analyze Resume"
5. Get your match score and recommendations in 30 seconds

- Job seekers optimizing resumes for specific positions

- Recent graduates entering the job market

- **Match Score**: Overall compatibility percentage

## Privacy

- Your resume is analyzed in real-time
- No data is stored on servers

## Tips for Best Results

- Use the complete job description
- Ensure your resume is text-based (not scanned)
- Include all relevant sections (experience, skills, education)
- Upload the most recent version of your resume
